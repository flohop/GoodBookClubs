About this site

This website is for everyone who likes books. You can share your progress on a book, discuss certain books, or talk about a whole genre! You can add new books and create new clubs. This site is a work in progress and I'm planning on adding new features over time. If you want a feature, or find a bug that's annoying you, feel free to let me know, I'd love to get your feedback. You can contact me under hoppe.florian2002@gmail.com.

About your data

If you've read the previous sections on this page, you have probably figured out by now, that this is a one man project, created by an 18-year-old in the attic of his parents . And I don't have any interest, or any use for your data. The only thing this website knows about you, is your username, the clubs your're in, and the books you've read. I will not do anything with that data. I wont sell it or give it to anyone! I am doing my best to protect the little data I have about you, but because I'm not a professional, please don't enter any sensitive data on this site because I can't guarantee to 100% that I will be able to protect it from any attacks.
