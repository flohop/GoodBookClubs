About this site

This website is for everyone who likes books. You can share your progress on a book, discuss certain books, or talk about a whole genre! You can add new books and create new clubs. This site is a work in progress and I'm planning on adding new features over time. If you want a feature, or find a bug that's annoying you, feel free to let me know, I'd love to get your feedback. You can contact me under hoppe.florian2002@gmail.com.
